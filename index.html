Tasbeeh Counter

You can save your tesbihat with Tasbeeh Counter application. You can use it on your mobile device. Tasbeeh Counter application will record all your Tasbihs.

Even if you close the Digital Tasbeeh Counter, the counter number will not be erased. When you restart the application, the tasbeeh number will appear on the screen. You should press the button to increment the number.

You can use the vibration from the buttons on the top of the screen. Additionally, to save battery, you can use the application in night mode. Tasbeeh Counter is free, and you won't see any advertisements during dhikr.

Specifications

  • Even if you close the application, you never lose the numbers of the tasbeeh until you press the pulse button.
  • The application will save energy with the help of the night mode option.
  • You can use the vibration to tasbeeh without checking the screen (you can disable the vibration).
